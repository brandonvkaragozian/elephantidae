ENHANCED MIXED INTEGER PROGRAM FOR CAMERA PLACEMENT
================================================================================

PROBLEM FORMULATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Decision Variables:
  x[j] ∈ {0,1}         : Binary indicator for camera at location j
  y[i] ∈ [0,1]         : Demand coverage of cell i
  z[i] ∈ [0,1]         : Geographic coverage of cell i

Objective Function:
  maximize: 0.6 * Σ_i (demand[i] * y[i]) +
            0.3 * Σ_i z[i] +
            0.1 * Σ_j (camera_value[j] * x[j])

Constraints:
  1. Budget (HARD): Σ_j x[j] = 16 (exactly 16 cameras)
  2. Demand: y[i] ≤ Σ_j detectability[i,j] * x[j]  ∀i
  3. Geographic: z[i] ≤ Σ_j detectability[i,j] * x[j]  ∀i

COMPONENT DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Demand (Cell Importance):
  Formula: 0.5*visits + 0.3*trajectories + 0.2*entries
           then boosted by 0.2*point_density
  Range: [0, 1] normalized
  Mean: 0.093
  High-demand (>0.5): 53 cells

Detectability (Camera→Cell Visibility):
  Formula: 1 - occlusion_factor (line-of-sight interpolation)
  Binary: >0.3 threshold
  Mean (binary): 243.3 cells per camera

Camera Value (Placement Priority):
  Formula: 0.7*demand + 0.3*diversity_bonus
  Diversity: Distance to 50 nearest cells (encourages spread)
  Purpose: Balance targeting activity + spatial redundancy
